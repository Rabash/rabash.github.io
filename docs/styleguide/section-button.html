Section 4 button Buttons

In addition to the <button> and <input type="submit|image|reset|button"> elements, the .c-button class and its variants can apply buttons styles to various elements (like an <a> link).

Parameters:
  • :disabled
    Disabled

Section 4.1 button.cta CTA button

CTA buttons are for call to actions.

Example 
<button class="c-button-cta ">Button</button>
